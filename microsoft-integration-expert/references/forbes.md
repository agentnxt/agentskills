## 📈 Forbes Coverage: Microsoft Business Highlights (2025)

Key Forbes-covered Microsoft business events:

### AI & Cloud
- **Azure revenue surpassed $75B** (FY2025) — Forbes covered as historic milestone
- **AI annual run rate: $13B+** (+175% YoY) — fastest-growing segment
- **OpenAI partnership** — exclusive Azure API deal, Forbes covered as strategic masterstroke
- Microsoft AI business recognized as one of the most valuable AI plays globally

### Workforce
- **May 2025:** ~6,000+ layoffs (~3% of workforce)
- **July 2025:** ~9,000 layoffs — largest reduction in 2+ years; affected Xbox (830 Redmond jobs)
- Total FY2025 workforce adjustments: ~15,000+
- Skype shutdown: Announced Feb 28, 2025; shut May 5, 2025 — Forbes tech coverage
- Russia operations: Microsoft Rus LLC filed bankruptcy 2025

### Leadership
- **Satya Nadella FY2025 compensation: $96.5 million** (+22% YoY) — Forbes covered
  - ~$84M in stock awards + $9.5M cash incentives
  - Tied to MSFT stock performance (+23% in 2025)
- **Nadella employee relations:** September 2025 town hall — acknowledged need to "rebuild trust" post-layoffs

### Credit Rating
- Microsoft is one of **only two US companies with AAA credit rating** (S&P) — also Johnson & Johnson
- Forbes uses this as a mark of Microsoft's financial bedrock strength
